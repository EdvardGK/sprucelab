Grid system
13-column Fibonacci grid · gutter 13 · card padding 13. Horizontal splits use Fibonacci sums: 8+5, 3+5+5, 5+5+3. Vertical rhythm: 600 → 370 → 230 → 140 (each row ≈ φ-minor of the one above).
Slot aspect at ~1280px container
c5 × hero ≈ 1:1.18 (square)
c8 × hero ≈ 1.4:1 (square-ish)
c5 × row2 ≈ 1.4:1 (square-ish)
c3 × row3 ≈ 1.3:1 (square-ish)
c8 × row2 ≈ 2.2:1 (bar/list slot)
c8 × row3 ≈ 3.6:1 (linear strip)
c5 × row4 ≈ 3.6:1 (linear strip)
Treemap-friendly = aspect 1:1 to 4:3. Linear charts work in any wider slot.
